Enrollment Process

For First Years: SHS Report Card (SF9), Good Moral Certificate, PSA Birth Certificate, Marriage Certificate (if married), and for ALS Passers: A&E Certificate + Eligibility to College.

For Transferees: Transfer Credentials/TOR, Good Moral Certificate, RO Form 14.
Put everything in a long brown envelope. At the back, write your:

• Full name
• Program (e.g., BS DevCom)
• Session (Day or Evening)
• Contact number
After your documents are checked, go to the Dean's Office (3rd Floor, ST Building). Only qualified applicants will get their official ID number.
Check with the Dean's Office (3rd Floor, ST Building). Blocks are assigned randomly or by department.
After block assignment, go to the Registrar's Office (Ground Floor, New Admin Building). They'll issue your COR. That's your proof you're officially enrolled! ✅
Nope! Regular undergrad students are covered by the Free Higher Education Program (RA 10931). Just visit the Scholarship Office (2nd Floor, New Admin Building) to confirm.
Once you:

✔️ Submit your documents
✔️ Get your ID number
✔️ Finish medical clearance
✔️ Receive your schedule/block
✔️ Get your COR
✔️ Verify at CAS Office

🎉 Congratulations, you're officially part of CTU DevCom! 🌻📸
