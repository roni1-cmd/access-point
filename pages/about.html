Information Compass

Access Point serves as your comprehensive guide through your academic journey at CTU. We provide essential information and resources to help you navigate various processes and challenges.

What We Offer:

  • Enrollment Guidance: Step-by-step assistance with enrollment processes, requirements, and deadlines
  • Exam Preparation: Tips, strategies, and resources to help you excel in your examinations
  • Interview Support: Preparation guides for academic interviews, scholarship applications, and career opportunities
  • Academic Resources: Information about courses, faculty, facilities, and academic policies
  • Campus Navigation: Help finding your way around campus and understanding university procedures

Our platform was created by First Year Development Communication students to ensure that information dissemination reaches every student who needs it. We understand the challenges of being a student, and we're here to make your academic journey smoother.

Anonymous Drop Box

Sometimes you need a safe space to express your feelings without judgment. Our Anonymous Drop Box provides both online and physical options for you to share what's on your mind.

How It Works:

  • Online Drop Box: Submit your thoughts, concerns, or emotions through our secure online platform
  • Physical Drop Box: Use our physical drop boxes located around campus for handwritten submissions
  • Complete Anonymity: Your identity is never revealed or tracked
  • Safe Environment: All submissions are handled with care and confidentiality

Why Use the Drop Box:

We understand that students face various challenges - academic pressure, personal issues, social anxiety, or simply need someone to listen. The drop box allows you to:

  • Express emotions without fear of judgment
  • Share concerns that you might not feel comfortable discussing face-to-face
  • Release stress and emotional burden in a healthy way
  • Know that someone cares about your wellbeing

About Access Point

Good day! Welcome to Access Point, where the silent voices that need to be heard are validated and are always welcome.

Access Point is an online platform created by First Year Development Communication students to help first year and other students with Information Dissemination.

Our Story:

Access Point: Help Desk is an online platform that caters to different features designed specifically for the CTU student community. We recognized the need for a centralized, accessible resource where students could find support, information, and a sense of belonging.

What Makes Us Different:

We are students helping students. Our team understands the unique challenges, pressures, and experiences that come with university life because we live them too. This peer-to-peer approach ensures that our platform remains relevant, accessible, and truly helpful.

Thank you and once again, welcome to ACCESS POINT!

Help Center

Need assistance? Our Help Center provides comprehensive support for all your questions and concerns.

How to Get Help:

  • Browse Information: Use our Information Compass to find guides and resources
  • Submit Questions: Use our contact form for specific inquiries
  • Anonymous Support: Use the Drop Box for sensitive concerns
  • Community Support: Connect with fellow students and peer mentors

Common Topics:

  • Enrollment and registration procedures
  • Academic requirements and policies
  • Campus facilities and services
  • Student organizations and activities
  • Mental health and wellness resources
  • Career guidance and opportunities

Response Time:

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, please indicate the urgency in your message.

Contact Us

We're here to help! Reach out to us through any of the following methods:

Get in Touch:

  • Online Form: Submit your questions through our secure contact form
  • Anonymous Drop Box: For sensitive or personal matters
  • Campus Locations: Visit our physical drop box locations around CTU
  • Student Representatives: Connect with our peer support team

Office Hours:

Our online platform is available 24/7. Student representatives are typically available during regular school hours and can be reached for urgent matters.

Emergency Support:

For immediate mental health support or crisis situations, please contact the CTU Guidance Office or emergency services directly.

Remember: No question is too small, and no concern is insignificant. We're here to support you throughout your academic journey.
